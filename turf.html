Same idea as a medical X-ray. The imagery shows where the turf is stressed and exactly where to look. Your superintendent or grounds crew reads the cause and writes the prescription. I fly, process, and hand you a clear picture. You make the call.

What this is, and is not

What this is: turf-health imaging. What this is not: grade, drainage, or terrain work. That takes a licensed surveyor. If you need it, I will tell you straight and point you the right direction. I stay in my lane: what the canopy is telling you, flagged and located.

A person, not a platform. The subscription services sell you a dashboard. I fly your field on your schedule, walk you through what I am seeing, and you have my number. Local, on-demand, no contract to read the imagery.

Empty fields only, flown early morning or off-hours. Written permission required before any imagery is used publicly. Turf-health imaging only.
